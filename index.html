About this plan

Characters are ordered by frequency (most-used first) with a coherence bonus — each day's batch is grouped so characters share semantic or structural connections, making them easier to memorize together.

The coherence score (0–1) measures how semantically related the characters in a given day's batch are to each other. Higher means more tightly clustered; lower means more diverse.
